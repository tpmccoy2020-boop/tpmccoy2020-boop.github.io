§ 01About

Accounting that sits at the intersection of regulation and operations.

Since 2021, I've managed State and Federal funds at the Arizona Governor's Office — leading grant audit, multi-fund reconciliation, and compliance oversight across consecutive fiscal cycles.

My work translates GAAP, SAAM, and 2 CFR 200 requirements into day-to-day controls: drafting and reconciling contracts and purchase orders, executing full-cycle AP/AR, standardizing subrecipient disbursement templates, and running month- and year-end close.

I built the Governor's Office travel budget and policy framework — 95% of trips land under budget — and inherited an annual stakeholder event running at a chronic loss, which I turned into a self-funding surplus by Year 4 through vendor renegotiation and tightened cost-allocation. I rely on instruments over instinct: Power BI dashboards, Power Query transforms, Smartsheet trackers, and Excel models (SUMIFS, pivots, forecasting) that let decisions get made from the ledger, not around it.
