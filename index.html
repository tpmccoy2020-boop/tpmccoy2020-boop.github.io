§ 01About

A five-year track record of stewarding public funds with discipline.

Since 2021, I've managed billions in public funds at the Arizona Governor's Office — leading grant audits, multi-fund reconciliation, and compliance oversight across State and Federal fiscal cycles.

My work sits at the intersection of regulation and operations: I translate GAAP and SAAM requirements into day-to-day controls, draft and reconcile contracts and purchase orders, close months and years cleanly, and partner with State Agency Directors on budget strategy for underrepresented programs.

I built the travel budget and policy framework for the Governor's Office — 95% of trips land under budget — and inherited an annual stakeholder event running at a chronic loss, which I turned into a self-funding surplus by year four. I prefer instruments over instinct: Power BI dashboards, Smartsheet trackers, Access databases, and Excel models that let decisions get made from the ledger, not around it.
